Japan Rail Pass

Purchasing the Japan Rail Pass will save you a handsome sum of money for touring around Japan, it will also save you the confusion of purchasing tickets. The JR Pass makes trip planning less tedious as pass holders could take any Shinkansen (Bullet Train) that is operated by Japan Railway while their pass is active. The pass could be purchased prior to ones arrival in Japan.

Subways

Riding the subway will be an integral part of your touring around Japan. Although it may seem sophisticated, you will soon come to learn that the Japanese metro system is not only logical, but also the most convenient way to get around in Japan.


Tokyo Metro has kindly provided riders with pamphlets that will guide users from the point they purchase the ticket to when they get off the subway.
