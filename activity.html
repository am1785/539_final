Shopping / Souvenir

Yodobashi Camera (ヨドバシカメラ)

Yodobashi Camera offers a wide variety of goods, it is certainly more than a camera shop. Some claim it to be the place that gave birth to one of Japan's largest consumer electronic store. From household appliances to personal computers and of course, filmmaking equipment, whatever you need, Yodabashi Camera has it.

Festivals / Matsuri (祭り)

Summer Festivals

Summer festivals, also known as Natsu Matsuri, are held during the summer from June to August.


The specific times and themes depend on the town/community that organizes it every year, however the activities during such gatherings are somewhat identical from one festival to another.


If you know one is about to happen near you, join in on the festivities! There will be food stands that serve delicious Japanese street food, fun carnival games that takes skills to beat, as well as a lot of people wearing Yukatas!

Gaming

VR Zone Shinjuku

VR Zone Shinjuku is Japan's largest VR entertainment facility. Players can choose from a large selection of the newest VR games and immerse themselves in the virtual world at VR Zone Shinjuku.

Sightseeing

Various Destinations

At last, sightseeing is a great way to learn more about Japan's cultural heritage and broaden your perspectives.


While many may prioritize this aspect of touring a foreign country the most, there are already an endless supply of guides out there on where and how to tour the historical landmarks of Japan.


Research routes ahead of time, take pictures, and if you're lucky, listen in to what the tour guides have to lecture about these places. It will be a fun experience without a doubt!
